Solar System

The Solar System consists of the Sun and the celestial bodies , which are bound to each other in this system by the force of gravity . The planetary system is a group of celestial objects revolving around a star that are not other stars, such as planets , dwarf planets , natural satellites , asteroids , meteors , comets and celestial dust .Our solar system is made up of our sun and its planetary system . These bodies include eight planets , their 172 known satellites , five dwarf planets, and billions of smaller bodies. These small bodies in the asteroid , Icy Kuiper belt object, Comet , Ulkayen include dust between the planets.

Venus

Venus the sun second planet and every 224.7 Earth 's solar orbit in days.The planet is named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty . It is the brightest natural object in the night sky after the Moon . Its apparent magnitude reaches a level of -4.6 and is bright enough to cast a shadow. Since Venus is an inferior planet , it is never seen far from the Sun when viewed from Earth: its obtuse angleReaches a maximum of 47.8 degrees. Venus reaches its maximum brightness before sunrise or only briefly after sunset. This is the reason for which it has been referred to by ancient cultures as the morning star or the evening star.
